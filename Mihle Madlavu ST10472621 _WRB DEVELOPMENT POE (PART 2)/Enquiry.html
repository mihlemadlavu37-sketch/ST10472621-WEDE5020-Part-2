Online Order Enquiries

Game allows customers to place and track orders online through its website and mobile platform. If customers face challenges such as delays, payment issues, or missing items, they can log an enquiry directly on the website or through the customer service portal. This system ensures transparency and convenience in the online shopping process.

Product Availability

To make shopping easier, Game provides product availability checks online and in-store. Customers can search for an item and see if it is in stock at their nearest branch. This helps reduce wasted trips and supports efficient shopping planning.

Customer Support

Game offers dedicated customer support through multiple channels including a call centre, email, and live chat. Support staff assist with order queries, product details, warranty claims, and general enquiries. The goal is to maintain customer satisfaction and trust by ensuring every query is resolved quickly and professionally.
