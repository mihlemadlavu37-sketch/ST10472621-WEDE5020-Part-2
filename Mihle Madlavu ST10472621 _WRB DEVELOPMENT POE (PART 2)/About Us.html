About Game Retail Store

Company Overview

Game is a well-known discount retailer that operates across South Africa and other African countries. Established in 1970, Game has become a household name by offering a wide range of products including electronics, appliances, groceries, furniture, sports gear, and household essentials. The store is part of the Massmart group, which is majority owned by Walmart Inc. This backing provides Game with strong international expertise, while still focusing on the needs of African consumers.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be Africa's most trusted discount retailer by delivering value, variety, and accessibility to every customer. Game strives to maintain its reputation as a one-stop shop where shoppers can find everything from everyday groceries to big-ticket appliances under one roof.

Our Mission

To provide customers with quality products at affordable prices, supported by excellent service and convenient shopping experiences.

Careers at Game

Game offers career opportunities across retail, logistics, customer service, and management. As part of the Massmart group, Game provides training and development for employees, encouraging growth and career advancement. The company values teamwork, innovation, and customer-focused service. Employment opportunities range from in-store positions to corporate roles, making it an attractive employer for those passionate about retail and service excellence.
